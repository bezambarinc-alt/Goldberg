The Helios

A floral necklace — leaves of light around a sun.

At the centre of the Helios, a single fancy yellow cushion diamond is set in a tight bezel of brilliant-cut pavé — the sun. Fanning outward from the bezel, graduated pear and marquise diamonds form two broad, articulated fans that meet behind the throat — leaves of light opening from the centre.

Each leaf is hinged individually, so that when the necklace is worn the fans move with the wearer rather than lying flat. Turn, and the light turns with her.

A piece for a room lit only by candles. For a shoulder bare.
