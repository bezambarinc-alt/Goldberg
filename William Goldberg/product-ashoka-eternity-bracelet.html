Ashoka Eternity

A line without end — flexible, continuous, unseen at the joins.

A continuous line of Ashoka® diamonds, set one beside the next around the full circumference of the wrist. Each stone sits in an individual bezel hinged to its neighbour, so the bracelet flexes freely in every direction the hand can move.

No clasp on top, no visible joins from any angle — the eye follows the line all the way around and never finds where it ends. That is the eternity.

Worn, the piece shifts with the wrist rather than against it. Turn the hand and the line turns with you; every stone stays oriented toward the light.
