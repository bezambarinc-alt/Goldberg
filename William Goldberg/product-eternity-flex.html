Eternity Flex

A ring that bends with the finger — and closes again.

An eternity band engineered to flex — a continuous line of diamonds set in an articulated platinum band that opens slightly under pressure, eases over the knuckle, and settles back into a true circle at rest.

The flex mechanism is hidden beneath the stones and silent in the hand. Worn, the ring feels like a standard eternity; put on or taken off, it gives the finger a moment of relief without stretching the metal or loosening the stones.

For the hand that is particular about fit. For the eternity that shouldn't have to be resized.
