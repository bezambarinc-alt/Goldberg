The Duet

Two stones, one conversation.

Two Ashoka® diamonds of nearly equal weight set side by side in a bypass band — the classical toi et moi setting, rendered in the modern cut. Each stone holds its own orientation on the finger; neither defers to the other.

The bypass construction means the two shanks curve past each other at the gallery rather than meeting in a flat plane. Worn, the eye reads the pair as a pair — in conversation, not in hierarchy.

A ring for two of anything: two names on a document, two promises made at once, two halves of a whole.
