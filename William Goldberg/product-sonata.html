The Sonata

A solitaire built around a single note held.

The Sonata is the atelier's purest solitaire — a platinum six-prong setting designed around a 5.01-carat Ashoka® of exceptional character. Nothing is added. Nothing competes.

The prongs are hand-drawn to the finest profile the metal will hold without compromising security, then polished to a mirror finish so they catch and return the light of the stone rather than carrying any of their own. The basket beneath is open on all four sides so that light enters through the pavilion as freely as through the table.

The shank is knife-edge — a single clean line in cross-section — tapering from 2.2mm at the gallery to 1.8mm at the base of the finger. Worn, the ring reads as a stone balanced in air.

A ring for the moment a promise is made. Or the moment it is remembered.
