The Nocturne

A riviera drawn on the throat in forty-two points of light.

The Nocturne is a study in graduation — a classical riviera reconsidered through the Ashoka® cut. Forty-two elongated cushions, beginning at just over a quarter-carat at the clasp, rising by thirds across the throat, and closing at a 2.20-carat centre stone that rests precisely at the hollow of the collarbone.

Each stone is set in a low, east-west orientation that catches light along the length of its table rather than across its crown. Worn, this produces a long, slow-breathing shimmer instead of the quick sparkle of a traditional riviera — a shimmer closer to moonlight on water than to sunlight on glass.

The connecting links are platinum, engineered to sit flush against the skin so that the necklace reads as a single continuous line of stones with no visible chain. The clasp is hidden beneath the final stone at the nape.

A piece for evening. For black. For the room she walks into last.
