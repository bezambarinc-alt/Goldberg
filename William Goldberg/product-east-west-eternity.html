East West Eternity

Ashoka stones turned sideways — light travelling the length of the wrist.

Every Ashoka® in this eternity bracelet is set east-west — the long axis of each stone running along the length of the wrist rather than across it. The effect is architectural: the stones read as a single elongated ribbon of light rather than as a counted sequence of individual cuts.

The setting is low and east-west throughout, with each stone linked by an articulated hinge that follows the wrist in motion. Worn, the bracelet lies flat and reads long — lengthening the wrist visually the way a tennis bracelet never does.

A more modern cousin to the Ashoka Eternity: same flex, same full circle, but with the light travelling differently.
