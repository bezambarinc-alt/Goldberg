The Interlude Drops

A pause held at the length of a breath.

The drop companion to the Interlude Hoops — the same family, translated into length. An Ashoka® stud at the lobe gives way to a single pavé line that falls, catching the jaw on its way down.

The drop is articulated on a fine platinum pin that lets it swing as a pendulum rather than hang dead. Every step, every turn of the head, the line moves a fraction and throws light in a new direction.

Where the Hoops are the pause in a phrase, the Drops are the long note held at the end of it.
